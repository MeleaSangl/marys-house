What Does This Website Need To Accomplish





This shows my brain storming for who my audience is and what kinds of problems I could solve and how.



User Goal Action
Abused Women To escape their situation as safely as possible Adding a button that will allow women to escape the page quickly if they need to, add positive imagery and language
Donors To find a reliable charity to donate to to feel good or get advertising Create an 'About Us' page so that they can learn about Mary's House and those who have been helped through this charity.
Church Finding godly charities to support Creating a group volunteer button so they can help with events and also creating a downloadable pdf for churches.
Volunteers To find a place to donate their time or get hours for clubs Create a clear place for volunteers to go that will explain how they can help in person as there is not a page for this but rather just events for donations
Mary's House To help women escape their situation as safely as possible Creating a clean clear webpage that is easy to navigate, create a donation page that does not leave to paypal, creating a new logo for them that does not look like a stencil
