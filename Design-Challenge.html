Design Challenge

How To Rise To The Challege And Meet It Head On

What is the problem you’re trying to solve?

Give women a space they feel safe running to when escaping abusive situations.

Take a stab at framing it as a design question?

How can I create a website that makes abused women feel safe enough to get help from it?

Now, state the ultimate impact you’re trying to have.

I want to help this group help women, ultimately it would be amazing to eliminate abuse all together

What are some possible solutions to your problem?

Using images to instill hope and tranquility, Limiting fonts to add clarity and legibility, Limit colors to give the reader space, Create a logo that can be recognized, Create a bar at the bottom that can transfer a women to a different website if she feels unsafe

Finally, write down some of the context and constraints that you’re facing.

Time and covid will make this a lot more difficult especially since I am having very bad symptoms right now.

Does your original question need a tweak? Try again.

How can I create an effective website that makes abused women feel safe enough to get help from it and donors willing to support the cause?
